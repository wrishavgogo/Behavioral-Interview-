Why do they ask this? What does the Hiring Manager want to know?

This is about judgment and decision-making under uncertainty.

Specifically, this question probes whether you:

1. Use sound reasoning when you don’t have perfect information.

  a) Do you freeze up waiting for all the answers?
  b) Or can you make progress with partial data?

2. Balance risk and action.

  a) Are you able to weigh trade-offs?
  b) Can you quantify what you don’t know and still move forward?

3. Validate and adjust your assumptions.

  a) Do you test hypotheses as you go?
  b) Are you open to changing direction if new facts emerge?

4. Communicate transparently about uncertainty.

  a) Did you keep stakeholders informed about what was known and unknown?
