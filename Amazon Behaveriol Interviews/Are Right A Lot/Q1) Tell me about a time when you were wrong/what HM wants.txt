They are assessing whether you:

1. Have Self-Awareness

  a) Do you recognize that you can be wrong?
  b) Are you able to reflect honestly without defensiveness?

2. Take Ownership

  a) Do you accept responsibility, or do you blame others?
  b) Are you accountable for the outcomes of your decisions?

3. Demonstrate Humility

  a) Do you think you have all the answers, or are you willing to learn from mistakes?
  b) Adapt and Learn

4. When you discover you were wrong, do you correct course quickly?

  a) Do you use the experience to improve your judgment?
  b) Communicate Transparently
  c) Do you proactively inform stakeholders when things go off track?

5. Can you maintain trust even when you make mistakes?
